Moderate incontinence: more confidence in everyday life

Moderate incontinence can noticeably affect daily life, whether during a brisk walk, while laughing, or without warning. These moments can feel uncertain or frustrating, but you are not alone. With the right knowledge and products, it’s possible to regain control and confidence.

What is moderate incontinence?

Moderate incontinence refers to the unintentional loss of a larger amount of urine than in cases of light incontinence. It may occur during physical activity, when changing positions, or without any clear trigger. While it can vary from person to person, it generally goes beyond small dribbles and may require products with increased absorbency.

Suitable products for moderate incontinence

For moderate incontinence, we recommend highly absorbent pads and incontinence pants. These products combine comfort, secure fit, and reliable discretion. Pants are especially helpful for active users, as they allow freedom of movement while offering reliable protection throughout the day. Pads with a higher absorbency level may also be a suitable option, depending on individual needs and lifestyle.

Help and treatment options

There are effective ways to manage moderate incontinence. Physiotherapy – particularly pelvic floor training – can be very effective. Depending on the cause, your doctor might also recommend medications or other interventions. A combined approach using therapy and appropriate products often brings the best results. A medical check-up is always recommended.

Tips for everyday life with moderate incontinence

Here are some practical tips for feeling more secure in daily life:

  • Wear clothing that makes changing easier and keeps products discreet.
  • Plan your daily routines with restroom access in mind.
  • Keep spare products in a discreet bag, for home, work or travel.
  • Choose products with odour control and breathable materials for added confidence.

With the right mindset and preparation, it is possible to go about your day with assurance and dignity.

Moderate Incontinence FAQ

What does "moderate" incontinence mean in everyday life?
It typically involves noticeable urine loss that may require changing products once or more throughout the day.

Which products offer reliable protection for several hours?
Absorbent incontinence pants and larger pads with high absorbency levels are ideal for extended protection.

How often should I change my incontinence pants?
This depends on the individual and the product used, but generally once every 4–6 hours is recommended for hygiene and comfort.

Can I combine pads and pants?
In some cases, yes - but it’s important to check compatibility, as not all products are designed to be used together.

Are there silent products for everyday use?
Yes, many modern products are designed with soft, rustle-free materials for discreet use in public or social settings.
