About Inkontinenz.de – Experience, Care and Confidence

Understanding Incontinence – with iD and Ontex as Your Reliable Partners

Inkontinenz.de is here to support anyone impacted by incontinence whether you're affected directly, or helping a loved one. Behind this platform stands Ontex, an international healthcare company with decades of experience in designing and producing absorbent hygiene products. Our incontinence brand, iD, is dedicated to helping people live safely and confidently, with dignity and discretion.

With iD, we focus on tailored product solutions and practical support. With Inkontinenz.de, we provide a direct gateway to everything you need to get started including personal consultation, a quick-start questionnaire, and product samples delivered directly from us as the manufacturer.

What Drives Us: Our Commitment

We believe in quiet confidence, honest support, and real care. At Inkontinenz.de, we don’t overpromise or overwhelm. We help people with practical solutions, human advice, and the respect every person deserves.

We exist to make life easier for people dealing with incontinence. That’s not a slogan, it’s our mission.

Who We Are – And What We Stand For

Ontex is a leading international manufacturer of personal hygiene products with over 40 years of experience. Our products are used daily by millions of people around the world in private homes, care institutions, and hospitals. From development and design to production and delivery, we take full responsibility for the products we offer. With our iD incontinence range, we bring together high-quality materials, advanced absorbent technologies, and the real-life experience of users across Europe.

We built Inkontinenz.de to be the direct point of contact for anyone in Germany who is starting to deal with incontinence with or without a prescription. It’s about making the first step easier, and every next one more supported.

Who We Support – And How

Our work is centered around real people and real needs. We support:

  • Individuals dealing with incontinence for the first time
  • Relatives and friends helping loved ones
  • Professionals in care and health settings

We offer practical tools to help -- including the Incontinence Coach, sample boxes, and direct communication. Our goal is to empower people to find the right solution for their needs and live with confidence.
