What Exactly Is Incontinence – and How Common Is It?

Incontinence is the unintentional loss of urine or stool. It affects people of all ages and has various causes -- from weakened pelvic floor muscles to chronic illnesses. It's more common than you think, and nothing to be ashamed of.
