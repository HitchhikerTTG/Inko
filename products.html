Which Products Fit Which Needs?

Products for Light Incontinence

Thin pads or panty liners with low absorbency. Ideal for occasional dribbling or light leakage. Discreet, comfortable, and suitable for everyday use.

Products for Moderate Incontinence

Pads or pants with medium absorbency. Suitable for recurring or uncontrolled urine loss. Combines protection with freedom of movement.

Products for Heavy Incontinence

Highly absorbent pants or shaped pads with fixation. For full bladder emptying or high care needs. Designed for security and comfort -- even over longer wear time.

Products for Children

Child-friendly incontinence products in suitable sizes. Designed for discretion and comfort. Note: Our core focus remains on adult care.
